Drag to orbit — while the camera moves you see the plain raster render. Let go and the path tracer accumulates samples; when it finishes (or on every sample, with the checkbox) the denoised result fades in on top. The slider reveals raw vs denoised.

The view selector shows the denoiser's inputs exactly as the network sees them (color with its flip applied; normals mapped [-1,1] → display). Append ?inspector to the URL for the three.js Inspector (render targets, params, profiler).
